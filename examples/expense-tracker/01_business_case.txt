Build a B2B SaaS platform where companies can manage employee expense reports.

Employees can:
- Submit expense reports with line items and receipt photos
- Categorize expenses (travel, meals, accommodation, equipment)
- Track the status of their submissions

Managers can:
- Review and approve or reject expense reports
- Set monthly budgets per team and department
- See spending trends and export reports

Finance team can:
- Process approved reimbursements
- Connect to accounting software (QuickBooks, Xero)
- Export transaction data as CSV

All users:
- Login with email/password or SSO (Google, Microsoft)
- Receive notifications when their report status changes
- Access via web app and mobile-friendly API

The system must support multi-tenancy: each company's data is completely isolated.
